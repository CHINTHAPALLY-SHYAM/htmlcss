    Green energy is energy that comes from natural resources like sunlight, wind, and water. It's also known as renewable energy. Green energy is produced in ways that don't harm the environment.

    "Green electricity" services refer to the provision of electricity generated from renewable energy sources like solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and biomass, offering a cleaner alternative to fossil fuels with reduced environmental impact.
